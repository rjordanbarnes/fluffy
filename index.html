About Us

Fluffy Kitties is a guild that was established in 2014 with hopes of bringing in new and experienced players alike and giving them a community where connections and life long friendships could be made.

Within the guild, Fluffy Kitties has also established a semi-hardcore raiding team currently progressing in Mythic HFC. A Heroic team is also beginning to form.

So whether you're looking to make new friends to grind achievements with, join our Mythic/Heroic raiding team, or even form a group for some RBGS and CM dungeons, Fluffy Kitties welcomes you.
