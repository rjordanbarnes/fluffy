Which team is best for you?

While Fluffy Kitties just began semi-hardcore raiding during the middle of HFC, raiding only 6 hours a week, we would like to kick it up and start off stronger than ever for Legion.

The level of time and commitment you have to offer will determine which team you are better suited for.

The 9 hour team will push harder and faster, while the 6 hour team will continue to push just as hard, regardless of them raiding 3 hours a week less. We understand not everyone can raid into the late evening hours, but still aspire to be on a successful team.

Both teams will be raiding mythic.
